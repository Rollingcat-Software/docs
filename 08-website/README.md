## Module Purpose

The FIVUCSAS landing website serves as:

1. **Introduction Portal** - First touchpoint for potential users and stakeholders
2. **Brand Identity** - Establishes visual and messaging identity
3. **Information Hub** - Explains platform capabilities and benefits
4. **Lead Generation** - Captures interest and facilitates onboarding
5. **Trust Builder** - Showcases technology, security, and compliance
